About Us

We are a team of students who recognize how hard it may be to find access to good resources, so we've put put together a collection of resources and advice on this site. The topics pertain to productivity, health and wellness, as well as setting personal goals, but we hope to expand the list very soon! In September, we'll have a blog page, so stay tuned! We are currently in the process of creating apps to assist students for a variety of purposes.
